Growing Greener Gardening App

In 2016 I took a UX Design course through Springboard.com. For the course I created an app idea and then went through the steps that a UX designer would take to go from ideation to market.

I decided to focus on an app to help guide beginners with an interest in gardening. Over the course of 16 weeks I did competitor research, created surveys, and conducted interviews to help refine my idea and create a viable solution.

See below for some of the documents and insights I gathered throughout the course.
